Enterprise Miss March the 23d 1877
mr James Hill

Dear sir

In according to my understanding Thire will be and Election held this fall for the perpose of Electing state and county officers

Thire will be a new board of Registrars appointed as the Democrats in this County are calling thire men together it behovvets us to commerce work for the future I wish to be appointed on the Board of Registration

I have ben at work for my Party and my Calor I wished to be place in a posision to work l more favorablely

I was in hast to write this letter and for most Excuse those bad spells words and writing so ill close for the Present

I Exspect your
Immediately reply

I am yours hunble sert.

David D Lewis
