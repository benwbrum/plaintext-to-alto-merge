Jackson Miss
July 31st 1876.

Col' Kinloch Falconer
Private Secretary
Jackson

Dr Sir:

I have the honor to return hereunto with my recommendation endorsed thereon the application of Matthew Gorman for commutation of sentence in his case. I think the judgment of the court was a little severe, and hence have recommen=ded a commutation of the sentence to three years.

I have the honor to be

Very Respectfully

R. V. Booth
Dist' Atty
