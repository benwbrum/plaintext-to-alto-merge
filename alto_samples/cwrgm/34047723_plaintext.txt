YAZOO COUNTY

Yazoo City, Miss. July 22d 1876

His Excellency J. M. Stone

Dear Sir

I enclose herewith Simeon Harrsons commission. In having declined to act as Registrar, & also inclose you a recommendation for the appoint-ment of J R Bell in his seat

Respectfully yours &c

Geo M Powell
Chancery Clerk
